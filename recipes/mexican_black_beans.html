Mexican Black Beans

Description

Ingredients

  • 2 tsp Oil
  • 1 cup chopped onion
  • 6 cloves garlic minced
  • 2 (16 oz) cans black beans not drained
  • 1/2 cup cilantro chopped
  • 2 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp salt

Steps

  1. In a small sauce pan, heat the oil over medium heat. Add the onions and garlic and cook 3-4 minutes, just until the onions begin to soften.
  2. Add the undrained black beans, cilantro, cumin, and salt. Stir well and reduce heat to medium low. Allow to cook for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally. Taste and adjust seasonings to your liking.
  3. Serve springled with more chopped cilantro and cotija cheese if desired.
