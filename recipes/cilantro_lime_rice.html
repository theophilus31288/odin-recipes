Cilantro Lime Rice

Description

Ingredients

  • 2 cup Brown Rice
  • 2.5 cup Broth or water
  • 1 tsp Salt
  • 1 Tbps Oil
  • 1/2 cup Cilantro chopped
  • 3 tsp Lime Juice
  • Lime zest

Steps

  1. Rince the brown rice and add it to the Instant Pot
  2. Add water or broth, oil and salt to the Instant Pot. Close the lid with vent in sealing position
  3. Cook on Manual High Pressure for 22 minutes. When the Instant Pot beeps, let the pressure release naturally for 10 minute, then manually release the pressure.
  4. Add the fresh lime juice, zest, and cilantro into the rice. Gently mix the rice and serve.
